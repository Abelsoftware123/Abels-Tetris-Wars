Rules

Use the **arrow keys** on your keyboard to control the falling blocks.

  • **UP** arrow to rotate the tetromino.
  • **LEFT** and **RIGHT** arrows to move the tetromino across the board.
  • **DOWN** arrow to speed up the tetromino's fall.

The goal is to complete horizontal lines of blocks without any gaps. When a line is complete, it will disappear, and you will earn points. The game ends when the blocks stack up to the top of the grid.
